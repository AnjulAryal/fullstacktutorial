Wai Wai Recipe (Nepali Brand Noddles)

A quick, simple and delicious Nepali Noodles

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Wai Wai noodle
  • chilly
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Turmeric

Steps

  1. Put Oil on kettle and add fry fine cutted onion till it turns in brown
  2. Add Salt and turmeric
  3. Add chachau and fry till it get brown
  4. add water and boil it for 5 minutes and chauchau is ready to serve for you
